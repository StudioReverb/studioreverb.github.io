Distill

Your bookmarks, purified.

Stop drowning in digital noise. Distill boils your bookmarking experience down to its most potent elements. We have stripped away the heavy folders and complex dashboards to give you a simple, filterable stream of your most important links. With frictionless inline editing and deletion, managing your web habits becomes a moment of zen rather than a chore.

Vinyl Vibe

In Progress

Vinyl Vibe is a social playground for crate diggers. We’ve turned collecting into a game where you showcase your heavy hitters and try to match them with other users' libraries. It's pretty simple: the more records you match, the more power you get to curate and style your own personal collection. With built-in chat to talk shop, it’s all about connecting over the music you love—minus the dust.

About Studio Reverb

Think of Studio Reverb as a digital sandbox. It’s a creative experiment where I mix design and development with a little help from AI. The goal isn't just to write code, but to see what’s possible when you let modern tools handle the heavy lifting. It's all about building cool, polished web experiences that focus on the vibe rather than the complexity.
